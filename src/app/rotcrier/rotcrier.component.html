rotcrier

A place for people who give a shit.
rotcrier.com is a place to shed light on issues, and for others to show they can give a shit.
back-end
  • The back-end is built using Nodejs, express, posgresql, express-session, sequelize, and express-session-sequelize.
  • It features a custom user encryption passowrd salt and encryption system for secure password storage.
  • It includes session management to provide secure modification of records, and allow users to remain logged in for the session duration.
  • Prior to modification of database records session information is compared to database records to determine if that request should be fulfilled or rejected.
  • Implements https enforcement, redirecting http traffic to https instead.
front-end
  • The front-end is built using a mixture of React, react-boostrap, and regular bootstrap. It also utilizes html-react-parser for rendering of links in posts displayed.
  • Users can create accounts and login using the user controls at the top of the page.
  • Users can create sections for community management. Other users can then join those sections and posts in that section.
  • Users can create posts in sections to bring attention to things they want to bring attention to. Other users can then show they give a shit, or share their thoughts and feelings in comment.
